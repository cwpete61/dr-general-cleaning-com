About DR General Cleaning

Founded in 2015, DR General Cleaning began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional cleaning services while prioritizing environmental responsibility and customer satisfaction. Our journey started with a small team dedicated to delivering superior results, and we've grown to become a trusted name in professional cleaning services.

Today, we continue to uphold our core values of integrity, excellence, and sustainability. Our team of certified professionals uses cutting-edge techniques and eco-friendly products to transform spaces while maintaining the highest standards of service and environmental stewardship.

Frequently Asked Questions

What cleaning products do you use?

We exclusively use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for children, pets, and the environment while maintaining professional-grade effectiveness.

How do you price your services?

Our pricing is based on the size of your space, cleaning requirements, and frequency of service. We provide detailed quotes after a brief consultation.

Are your cleaning staff insured and bonded?

Yes, all our staff members are fully insured, bonded, and undergo thorough background checks for your peace of mind.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Last-minute cancellations may incur a small fee to cover our staff's scheduled time.

Do you guarantee your work?

Absolutely! We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll return to address any concerns at no additional cost.
